Core RPG Experience

Your adventure begins somewhere at a wildland. This place is called Avalon...


The Begining

Every adventurer searches his path to step on.
Some of them are searching for power whose fighting each other with powerful spells, ancient blades and inevitable arrows.
Some looks for justice so using their powers to fight with army of monsters.
The weak unite to become stronger. Their towns grow.
The wise are looking for knowledge. The Greedy are using it to get rich.
Rest of them want to live a simple life but is working hard to reach their goals.
All of them want to survive.

Which path will you choose, Adventurer?

Custom Items

Find Them

Discover over two hundred unique craftable items. Choose your weapon from a huge collection of crossbows, daggers, throwable spears, magic staffs, battle axes and others.


Necessary Tools

Exploring the world will let find a lot of rare materials.
Well used will allow to create powerful artifacts or upgrade adventurers equipment.
Common materials can be used in a different way than originally. Merging them allows to create better ones.
Explore the world and kill monsters searching these items.

Use everything you get to survive in this dangerous world.
